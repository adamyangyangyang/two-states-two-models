C — Why demographics matter for schools

To write Do not treat demographics as decoration. Connect each demographic fact to schooling: • poverty → student need, staffing costs, program costs
• language diversity → English-learner programs, bilingual staffing
• enrollment change → budget pressure (declining enrollment shrinks budgets in some formulas; growth strains facilities)
• concentration → segregation effects 1–2 paragraphs.
